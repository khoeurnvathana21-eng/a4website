Who We Are

Built for
Cambodian Players

athana Football Boots is a football boot store in Cambodia. Our goal is simple: to help Cambodian players find quality football boots from top brands at fair prices. We are committed to providing trusted products, great service, and fast delivery across the country.

We carry only authentic Nike and Adidas products — the newest 2026 models, sourced directly and priced fairly for the Cambodian market. No fakes. No outdated stock.

Whether you are a Sunday-league amateur or a competitive club player, you deserve boots that perform. That is what we sell.
